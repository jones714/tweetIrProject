 Topic Selection

The Idea is to gather tweets from Twitter related to China/Wuhan and the Corona Virus. Our Analysis wil determine whether such tweets are positive or negative.

Dataset Collection

As mentioned, the data collected from the internet will be tweets from Twitter. This data will come from relevant tweets centered upon topics from hashtags such as: #China #Wuhan #CoronaVirus #COVID19

Implementation

The Project will be implemented using Python and Flask.  Data will be organized and preprocessed using Python libraries such as NLTK. Once our system determines whether a tweet is good or bad, the final analysis will be represented in graphs/charts.
